Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information while providing a safe coloring experience—especially for children.
The App complies with COPPA, GDPR, and Google Play policies.

Name

Age

Phone number

Email

Photos or videos

Contacts

Device files

Location

Payment information

Login data

The App does not require registration or an account.

3. Third-Party Data Collection (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads.

AdMob may automatically collect non-personal data, such as:

Advertising ID

App usage data

Interaction with ads

We do not have access to this data.
AdMob uses it only for:

Delivering ads

Frequency capping

Fraud prevention

Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Since this App is designed for children:

We do not collect personal information from children under 13.

We use child-safe non-personalized ads.

No sensitive content is shown.

Parents may contact us to request removal of any accidentally collected data.

If you believe we have mistakenly collected a child’s personal information, email us at
appcraftinfotech@gmail.com

and we will delete it immediately.

5. How We Use Information

We do not store or process personal data.

Showing ads

Improving ad quality

Ensuring user safety

Only Google (AdMob) handles automatic non-personal ad data according to their privacy policies.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information.
